PRIVACY & PERMISSIONS SUMMARY
================================================================================

REQUIRED PERMISSIONS:

Location Services (NSLocationWhenInUseUsageDescription):
  Purpose: "WilderGo needs your location for safety features, emergency SOS
  tracking, and community discovery. Your location is used for crash/fall
  detection, emergency response routing, and nomadic community connections."
  
  Privacy Details:
  - Location data is captured at high accuracy (bestForNavigation) when app
    is in use
  - Used for emergency SOS payloads stored locally on device
  - Shared with emergency responders ONLY during active SOS event
  - Can be disabled in Settings > Privacy > Location Services

Camera (NSCameraUsageDescription):
  Purpose: "WilderGo uses camera for selfie verification during account setup
  and community profile verification."
  
  Privacy Details:
  - Camera is only accessed during selfie verification step in onboarding
  - Photos are processed locally and uploaded ONLY after user confirmation
  - Users can deny camera permission and skip verification
  - No background camera access

Microphone (NSMicrophoneUsageDescription):
  Purpose: "WilderGo may request microphone access for video messaging features."
  
  Health & Safety Kit (SafetyKit):
  - Crash detection (iOS 17+): Detects vehicle accidents, triggers emergency SOS
  - Fall detection (iOS 17+): Detects user falls, initiates safety check workflow
  - No personal health data is logged; only emergency events trigger actions
